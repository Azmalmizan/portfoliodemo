About Me

I am an a student of University of Asia Pacific. I earned by Ph.D. in Computer Science from Georgia Tech. I was fortunate to be supervised by professor Wenke Lee and Taesoo Kim.

My research interests include system security, program analysis and verification, and operating systems. My current research focuses on vulnerability related topics, including

  • Advancing techniques for finding vulnerabilities in binaries, OS kernels, machine learning, and cyber-physical systems.
  • Eliminating vulnerabilities through automatic patch generation and verification.
  • New exploit techniques and automated exploit generation.
  • Runtime exploit prevention with software hardware co-design.
